Fated Finds

Fated Finds is an ongoing mini series where I spotlight independent creatives, small shops, and the one-of-one pieces that carry stories with them. Each episode follows a “found” object or person and traces how it landed exactly where it was meant to be.

Through portraits, texture-heavy b-roll, and conversational storytelling, I’m exploring how chance, community, and curiosity intersect. This series is my way of giving these people and pieces a little cinematic care — even if it’s just for 60 seconds at a time.
